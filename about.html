Welcome to the divine curation. This site is primarily a blog about philosophy, media, and culture, though it also serves as a repository where I collect various pieces of work, including longer form writings published elsewhere, and projects in other media.

This is a static site created using Jekyll that runs on Github Pages. This is great because it has no ads, costs nothing, runs (almost) no javascript, collects zero stats, and gives me complete control of the functionality, structure, and appearance of the site.

If you would like to contact me I can be emailed at divinecurationsite [at] gmail [dot] com. I’m also on Twitter, and have other bits scattered around the web at Medium, Soundcloud, Mixcloud, and Vimeo.
